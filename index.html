About Neiro

​Neiro is a community-driven project on the Base blockchain, embodying the principles of decentralization and collaboration. As a proud partner of Sheertopia and Based Africa, Neiro fosters a vibrant ecosystem where members collectively contribute to its growth and success. With a commitment to transparency and inclusivity, Neiro empowers individuals to shape the future of its platform, ensuring that the community remains at the heart of all endeavors.​
